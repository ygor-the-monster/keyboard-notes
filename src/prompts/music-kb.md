MUSIC THEORY REFERENCE (ground your answers in this):

NOTES & PITCH
- 12 chromatic pitches: C C#/Db D D#/Eb E F F#/Gb G G#/Ab A A#/Bb B, then repeat an octave higher.
- Sharp (#) raises a semitone; flat (b) lowers a semitone; natural cancels. Enharmonic = same pitch, different name (C# = Db).
- An octave = 12 semitones. A whole tone = 2 semitones.

INTERVALS (semitones): unison 0, m2 1, M2 2, m3 3, M3 4, P4 5, tritone 6, P5 7, m6 8, M6 9, m7 10, M7 11, octave 12.

SCALES
- Major scale pattern (whole/half steps): W W H W W W H. C major = C D E F G A B.
- Natural minor: W H W W H W W. A minor = A B C D E F G (relative minor of C major).
- Harmonic minor raises the 7th; melodic minor raises 6th & 7th ascending.
- Major and its relative minor share a key signature; the minor starts on the 6th degree of the major.

KEY SIGNATURES / CIRCLE OF FIFTHS
- Sharps order: F C G D A E B ("Father Charles Goes Down And Ends Battle"). Flats: the reverse.
- Sharp keys by fifths up from C: G(1#) D(2#) A(3#) E(4#) B(5#) F#(6#). Flat keys by fourths up: F(1b) Bb(2b) Eb(3b) Ab(4b) Db(5b) Gb(6b).

CHORDS
- Triads stack thirds: major (root, M3, P5), minor (root, m3, P5), diminished (m3, dim5), augmented (M3, aug5).
- Sevenths: dominant 7 (major triad + m7), major 7 (major triad + M7), minor 7 (minor triad + m7).
- Diatonic triads of a major key (Roman numerals): I ii iii IV V vi vii°. Of natural minor: i ii° III iv v VI VII.

COMMON PROGRESSIONS
- I–IV–V(–I), I–V–vi–IV (pop), ii–V–I (jazz cadence), I–vi–IV–V (50s), 12-bar blues (I–I–I–I–IV–IV–I–I–V–IV–I–V).

RHYTHM & TIME
- Time signature: top = beats per bar, bottom = beat unit (4 = quarter note). 4/4 common time, 3/4 waltz, 6/8 compound duple.
- Note durations: whole, half, quarter, eighth, sixteenth (each half the previous). A dot adds half the value again.
- Tempo (BPM): Largo ~50, Adagio ~70, Andante ~90, Moderato ~110, Allegro ~130, Presto ~180.

DYNAMICS (soft→loud): pp, p, mp, mf, f, ff. Crescendo = get louder; diminuendo/decrescendo = get softer.
ARTICULATION: staccato (short), legato (smooth/connected), accent (emphasize), tenuto (full value), fermata (hold).

ABC NOTATION (this app engraves staves from ABC)
- Header fields: X: (tune number), T: (title), M: (metre, e.g. 4/4), L: (default note length, e.g. 1/4), K: (key, e.g. C).
- Notes: C D E F G A B (one octave), c..b (octave up), C,/c' shift octaves. Numbers set length (C2 = double), / halves. | = barline. z = rest.
